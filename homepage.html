TCU reporting students from the Department of Journalism devoted time researching different elements of the TCU Factbook to gain more knowledge of campus diversity and how it has impacted factors including admission over the course of TCU's existence. Students were divided into groups to compile their research from facts and figures in order to provide visual aids reflecting their findings. Students took an insightful look at patterns and trends, and also learned how to nagivate through the information provided by the university's factbook.
