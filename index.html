  • About this site

TCU reporting students from the Department of Journalism devoted time researching different statistics provided by the TCU Factbook to gain more knowledge of the university. Students were divided into groups to compile their research and create visual aids reflecting their findings. Students took an insightful look at patterns and trends, and also learned how to nagivate through the information provided by the university's factbook.

Fab Five: Athletic Scholarships

Members: Meagan Thompson, Caleb Jakana, China Perkins, Sarah Jane Grisham, Danielle Johnston

Because of Title IX, there has to be an equal number of men and women athletic scholarships to each other, but for sports that are not parallel they, essentially, have no limits. Sports that receive more money for the university tend to have more high-end, expensive facilities, especially non-parallel sports.
