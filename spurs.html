Female Faculty

Despite the fact the College of Science and Engineering and the Neeley School of Business have had a subtle increase in female faculty over the past five years, they were under 40 percent of the total faculty; however, the percentage of female faculty in the Harris College of Nursing and Health Sciences and the College of Education has stayed over 70 percent.

Numbers for female faculty have remained relatively stable since 2011 but the numbers for the Neeley School of Business and the College of Science and Engineering have slightly increased over 5 years. In the College of Nursing and Health Sciences and the College of Education the percentages for female faculty have stayed over 70 percent. The Bob Schieffer College of Communication and Addran College of Liberal Arts have held relatively equal percentages between male and female faculty with a 60:40 ratio, but male faculty still outnumber female faculty.
