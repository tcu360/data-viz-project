Student-Athlete Graduation Success Rate

Our project shows the data between student-athletes and graduation success rate. The calculations are displayed below in the bar graph. The sports analyzed include football, basketball, and baseball in the Big XII Conference for the school year of 2015-2016. The student-athlete graduation rate is calculated on the Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System-GSR. This is the proportion of the first-year, full-time student-athletes who enter a school on athletic aid and graduate within six years. The GSR does not penalize colleges for student-athletes who transfer in good academic standing.

In comparison to other Big XII Conference schools, TCU has the second highest GSR for football at 79%, behind University of Kansas. TCU has a GSR of 69% for basketball, behind seven other Big XII schools. For baseball, TCU has a GSR of 60%, tied with West Virginia for the lowest GSR.
